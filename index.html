ABOUT ME

My name is Meenakshi Kohli, and I enjoy teaching and help students understand the underlying concept of a topic or a problem. My approach to tutoring is simple - I like to start teaching at the student level and give a student the freedom to ask as many questions as he/she wants. I have a Master's degree in Physics from Ohio University and working on my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering. I have my undergraduate with Physics Major and Mathematics minor. My experience includes teaching at various Community colleges as a Physics Lecturer for 5 years. While teaching Physics, I also taught algebra and calculus classes. I have over seven years of experience as a tutor from Grade 9 to Undergraduate students.
